### Four Contributions to VISSOFT 2025

The [Proceedings](https://dl.gi.de/handle/20.500.12116/47940) of the [16<sup>th</sup> Symposium on Software Performance (SSP 2025)](https://www.performance-symposium.org/ssp-2025/) are available, which took place in Kiel on November 4<sup>th</sup> and 5<sup>th</sup>, 2025, with the following contributions from our group:

- [Schnoor, Henning; Yang, Shinhyung: **16th Symposium on Software Performance 2025**](https://dl.gi.de/handle/20.500.12116/47963)
- [Larrivain, Daphné; Yang, Shinhyung; Hasselbring, Wilhelm: **Dynamic and Static Analysis of Python Software with Kieker**](https://dl.gi.de/handle/20.500.12116/47966)
- [Reichelt, David Georg; Yang, Shinhyung; Hasselbring, Wilhelm: **Interoperability From OpenTelemetry to Kieker: Demonstrated as Export from the Astronomy Shop**](https://dl.gi.de/handle/20.500.12116/47964)
- [Hansen, Malte; Reichelt, David Georg; Hasselbring, Wilhelm: **Analysis and Visualization of Unit Test Traces With Kieker and ExplorViz**](https://dl.gi.de/handle/20.500.12116/47950)
- [Yang, Shinhyung; Reichelt, David Georg; Ingo, Henrik; Hasselbring, Wilhelm: **Detection of Performance Changes in MooBench Results Using Nyrkiö on GitHub Actions**](https://dl.gi.de/handle/20.500.12116/47944)
