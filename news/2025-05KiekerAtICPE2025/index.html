The Kieker Observability Framework will be presented at ICPE 2025

With the latest inclusion of the Kieker Observability Framework in the SPEC RG repository,1 Shinhyung is provided with an opportunity to present Kieker in three different sessions at the upcoming ICPE 2025.2

  • Artifact HandsOn will give the participants a hands-on experience of the Kieker Observability Framework, through our tool artifact and trace analysis. The session is scheduled on Tuesday, May 6, 2025 at 14:00 h.

  • Poster Presentation will be held on Friday, May 9, 2025 at 12:30 h, during the lunch break. ICPE participants will be able to see the live demonstration of our tool artifact on a laptop along with the poster.
    • ICPE2025 Poster-Kieker Observability Framework
  • Pitch Presentation will be held on Friday, May 9, 2025 at 14:00 h, as a 5-minute talk. 3

The hands-on tool artifact is available in three formats: (1) Docker images on the Docker Hub registry with the Docker compose scripts on GitHub4, (2) a single archive file of all Docker image exports, and (3) a single virtual machine image that runs the tool artifact automatically at the start-up. (2) and (3) are uploaded to Zenodo.5 You may obtain the proceedings paper from ACM6 or a preprint from arXiv.7
